Earth Kingdom Warrior

Welcome!

This is an Avatar: The Last Airbender earthbending themed game.

Instructions

Don't touch the flying boulders and metal pieces! Use your earth bending powers to either push the boulders out of the way or to collect them as ammo.
Move around with the arrow keys
Press space to deflect the boulders!
Press z to collect the boulders as ammo!
